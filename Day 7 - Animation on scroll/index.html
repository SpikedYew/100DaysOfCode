1.Octopuses have three hearts


👀 – Two pump blood to the gills, while one pumps it to the rest of the body. They also have blue blood due to a copper-based molecule!

2.👁️ Honey never spoils


– Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible!

3.A day on Venus is longer than a year


– It takes Venus longer to rotate on its axis (about 243 Earth days) than it does to orbit the Sun (225 Earth days).

4.Bananas are berries


- Technically, bananas qualify as berries, while strawberries do not. Berries are defined as fruit produced from a single ovary.
